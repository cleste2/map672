Three Cities

Map authored by Cynde Estep

Three cities including their population.

This map identifies three cities in Kentucky of different sizes. Frankfort is the Capital of the Commonwealth. The city is located along the Kentucky River in Franklin County. Lexington is the city where I reside. It is the 2nd largest city in Kentucky and is known as the "Horse Captial of the World." It is also ranked 10th in the US in college educated residents with 39.5% of residents having a college degree. Lexington is located in Fayette County. The third most populated city in the state; Bowling Green, is where the Corvette Museum is located. It is located in Warren County and was the capital of Conferate Kentucky during the civil war.
